ABOUT

My name is Ryan, I am 20 years old, and I grew up in Buckland’s Beach, Auckland. I attended
to Maclean’s College, where I was a school and sports prefect, often organizing and running events
held by – and within – the school. I have just completed by Bachelors in Computer and Information

front-end, especially in the area of UX / UI Design and development. Through my work experience, I
have encountered the full agile software development lifecycle, and enjoy the challenges it presents
on a global scale. I am extremely keen to start my web development career, and become an expert in
technology related to front-end (JS, React, Angular etc). In my free-time I enjoy playing the guitar,
working out, and (to be honest) exploring development.
